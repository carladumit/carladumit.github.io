About me

Welcome to Carla's Reading Journal — my little corner of the internet dedicated to all things books. I've started this blog to document my reading journey, share my honest ratings and reviews, and track my reading stats along the way.

I'm especially passionate about Fantasy and Sci-Fi, so if you're looking for magical worlds, epic adventures, or futuristic stories, you’re in the right place. Here you'll find plenty of recommendations, thoughts, and discoveries from the genres I love most.

Whether you're searching for your next favourite book or just enjoy geeking out about stories, I'm happy to have you here. Let's read together!
